Overview

Purpose

To outline the goals, strategies, and tactics for selling books online, to guide the development and operation of the website and attract and retain customers and finally to achieve financial success.

Audience

Website Idea: Bookworm's Paradise

Why will people come to your site?

Loowis books is a website for book lovers of all ages. Our goal is to provide our visitors with the information and resources they need to discover new books, connect with other book lovers, and expand their literary horizons.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

Here are some of the questions that our visitors may expect our site to answer for them:

  • What are the latest book releases?
  • What are the best books to read in a particular genre?
  • What are some underrated or hidden gem books?
  • What are some good books for kids or teens?
  • What are some good books to read for a book club?
  • What are some good audiobooks to listen to?
  • Where can I find deals on books?

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

Here are some of the activities that our visitors may want to complete on our site:

  • Browse and search for books by genre, author, title, etc.
  • Read book reviews and ratings from other readers
  • Write their own book reviews
  • Create and share book lists
  • Join and participate in book clubs
  • Discover new authors and genres
  • Enter to win book giveaways
  • Purchase books from our online bookstore

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

To purchase books from our online bookstore, visitors will need to provide their shipping address and payment information. To join a book club, visitors will need to create an account and provide their name and email address. To write a book review, visitors will need to create an account and provide their name and email address.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Here are some of the things that will make Bookworm's Paradise stand out from other book websites:

  • We will offer a wide selection of books, including new releases, bestsellers, and classics.
  • We will offer competitive prices and discounts on books.
  • We will have a user-friendly and easy-to-navigate website.
  • We will offer a variety of features and resources for book lovers, such as book reviews, book lists, book clubs, and author interviews.
  • We will be committed to providing excellent customer service.

In addition to the above, here are some specific text content that our audience may want to read or get from a bookstore site:

  • Book excerpts
  • Author interviews
  • Book recommendations
  • Book reading guides
  • Book news and reviews

We believe that Loowis books will be a one-stop shop for book lovers of all ages. We will offer our visitors the information, resources, and community they need to discover new books, connect with other book lovers, and expand their literary horizons.
